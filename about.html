Our Story

30 Years of Building Visions

Founded in 1995 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, Vocto Technologies began as a specialist electronics manufacturing unit serving the rapidly growing Indian technology sector. From our earliest days, precision and reliability were non-negotiable — principles that remain central to everything we do today.

In the early 2000s, recognising the transformative potential of photonics, we expanded our capabilities to include advanced optical and photonics sub-assemblies. This move positioned us uniquely as a manufacturer capable of serving the most demanding segments — aerospace, defence, and scientific instrumentation.

Our MEPZ-SEZ certification in 2008 marked a strategic milestone, enabling streamlined export operations and signalling our readiness to serve global clients at scale. The partnership with HCL Technologies followed, further cementing our reputation for engineering excellence.

In 2018, we established our European presence through our operations in Finland, working alongside Fenno Aurum Oy. This office serves as the bridge between our Indian manufacturing capabilities and our growing European client base, combining cost-efficient production with European quality expectations.

Since 1995

Our Journey

Thirty years of milestones that have defined who we are and shaped the precision engineering we deliver today.

1995

Founded in Chennai

Vocto Technologies established as a precision electronics manufacturing unit in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, with a focus on reliability and quality for technology clients.

Early 2000s

Photonics Expansion

Expanded manufacturing capabilities to include advanced photonics sub-assembly and optical alignment, enabling entry into high-precision scientific and industrial markets.

2008

MEPZ-SEZ Certification

Achieved Madras Export Processing Zone (MEPZ) Special Economic Zone certification, enabling seamless export operations and unlocking access to global OEM clients.

2012

HCL Technologies Partnership

Established a strategic partnership with HCL Technologies, one of India's leading IT and engineering services companies, expanding our client base and technical capabilities.

2018

European Operations Established

Launched our European operations in Finland through partnership with Fenno Aurum Oy, providing local presence and support for European OEMs and technology companies.

2025

Cloud & SaaS Service Lines

Launching our fourth service line — Cloud Services (IaaS & SaaS) — bringing enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure and industrial software solutions to our engineering clients worldwide.
