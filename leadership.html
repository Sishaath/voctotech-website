Founder & Managing Director

R. Ramesh Kumar

Founder & Managing Director, Vocto Technologies

With over 30 years of experience in electronics and photonics manufacturing, R. Ramesh Kumar founded Vocto Technologies on a simple conviction: that precision and integrity, together, can build products that define industries. From a single manufacturing unit in Chennai, he has grown the company into a globally connected engineering services partner with operations in India and Finland.

His vision for Vocto Technologies has always extended beyond manufacturing — seeing the company as a bridge between the precision capabilities of South Asian engineering and the quality expectations of global markets. The expansion into design services, prototyping, and most recently cloud solutions reflects his commitment to evolving with the industries the company serves.

Under his leadership, Vocto Technologies achieved MEPZ-SEZ certification, built strategic partnerships with HCL Technologies and Fenno Aurum Oy, and established a reputation for delivering on the most demanding engineering challenges in aerospace, defence, healthcare, and photonics.
