Radhakrishnan R

Managing Director, Vocto Technologies

With over 30 years of experience in electronics and photonics manufacturing, Radhakrishnan R leads Vocto Technologies with a vision of precision, integrity, and global reach. From establishing the company as a MEPZ-SEZ certified unit in Chennai to building partnerships with HCL Technologies and expanding operations into Finland, he has steered Vocto into a globally connected engineering services partner trusted by leading OEMs and technology companies worldwide.

His leadership philosophy centres on delivering end-to-end engineering value — from design and prototyping through to full-scale manufacturing — while maintaining the highest standards of quality demanded by aerospace, defence, and healthcare markets.
