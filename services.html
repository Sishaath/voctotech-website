Our Service Lines

Four integrated service lines covering the complete product lifecycle — from initial concept and design through prototyping, precision manufacturing, and cloud deployment.

Service Line 01

Manufacturing & Engineering as a Service (MaaS)

Vocto Technologies delivers precision electronics and photonics manufacturing from our MEPZ-SEZ certified facility in Chennai. With over 30 years of manufacturing expertise, we serve global OEMs, defence contractors, aerospace companies and healthcare device manufacturers.

Our ISO-aligned processes and special economic zone certification ensure competitive pricing, export facilitation, and uncompromising quality across all production volumes — from NPI runs to high-volume manufacturing.

PCB Assembly — SMT and through-hole, mixed technology
Conformal coating and potting for harsh environments
Complete box-build and system-level integration
Photonics sub-assembly and optical alignment
Functional testing, ICT and AOI inspection
Supply chain management and component sourcing
MEPZ-SEZ Certified Defence Grade Photonics

Service Line 02

Design & Development as a Service (DDaaS)

Our engineering team provides complete hardware and firmware design services for complex electronics systems. From initial schematic capture through verified, production-ready PCB layouts, we deliver designs optimised for manufacturability, reliability, and regulatory compliance.

We specialise in high-speed digital designs, mixed-signal circuits, power electronics, and embedded firmware for demanding applications including aerospace, defence, medical devices and industrial control systems.

Schematic capture and multi-board hardware architecture
High-speed PCB layout with signal integrity analysis
Embedded firmware development (RTOS, bare-metal)
FPGA design, HDL coding and timing closure
Power supply design and thermal management
Design for manufacture (DFM) and test (DFT) reviews
Hardware Design Firmware FPGA

Service Line 03

Prototype Development as a Service (PDaaS)

Accelerate your product development cycle with Vocto's rapid prototyping capabilities. We build functional proof-of-concept units and engineering samples that de-risk your design before committing to volume production, saving time and cost at every stage.

Rapid PCB prototype builds — 24 to 72-hour turnaround
Design for Manufacture (DFM) analysis and reporting

Engineering model assembly and bring-up support
